AaronNewcomer.Net

Web hosting freebies.

This very website was a fun little project to see how many cloud services I could string together for some free web hosting. I also enjoy the irony of adding my portfolio site to my portfolio. Some of the highlights:

  • Site files are hosted in a public Google Drive folder. Google supports this.
  • Google Drive site URLs are ugly as sin, so I use a Google App Engine application with a much cleaner URL to serve as a reverse proxy using PHP cURL (actually, Google's cURL "lite"). This app also powers my contact form.
  • Google App Engine allows you to point domains directly at your app. Good-bye, //aaronnewcomernet.appspot.com... Hello, //aaronnewcomer.net!
  • I use Cloudflare for a DNS nameserver and reverse proxy. As a static site, I can maximize cache settings to save my bandwidth quota. 10/10, would recommend.
  • Site styling uses a free theme from startbootstrap.com with images from Death to the Stock Photo and (temporarily) Fill Murray/Fill Murray.
  • You're being watched by the eye in the sky, natch.

  • Date: August 2015
  • Client: AaronNewcomer.Net
